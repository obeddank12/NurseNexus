A-Z Index

  • Asthma

    Respiratory: Airway inflammation and bronchospasm.

  • COPD

    Respiratory: Chronic airflow obstruction (Emphysema/Bronchitis).

  • Diabetes Insipidus (DI)

    Endocrine: ADH deficiency and extreme polyuria.

  • Diabetes Mellitus (DM)

    Endocrine: Glucose metabolism and insulin issues.

  • Heart Failure (CHF)

    Cardiovascular: Pump failure and systemic/pulmonary congestion.

  • Hypertension (HTN)

    Cardiovascular: Persistent high blood pressure.

  • Stroke (CVA)

    Neurological: Acute brain attack.

Standard Lab Ranges

pH:7.35 - 7.45
PaCO2:35 - 45 mmHg
HCO3:22 - 26 mEq/L
Potassium:3.5 - 5.0 mEq/L
Sodium:135 - 145 mEq/L

*Essential values for Level 300 clinical rotations.
